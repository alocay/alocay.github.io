Notes on this design

  • Art row is visually dimmed (reduced opacity) — present but clearly secondary
  • Numbered rows (01–04) add editorial structure without being precious about it
  • The hyphenated name treatment on the left is expressive but can be adjusted
  • On hover, each nav row would highlight with an amber left-border flash
  • Clicking a row triggers a full-page slide transition to that section
